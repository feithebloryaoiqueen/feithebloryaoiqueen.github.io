Chapter 2

He thought a bit, before replying.

‘Oh hey, so you texted me just to tell me the party’s over, and that you wish I was there? That’s nice of you, but, me being at the party wouldn’t make a difference. No one notices me anyway’

He wiped out the last phrase and then added it back and hit send.

About 5 minutes later, Aiden replied to the text

‘WDYM “no one notices you”??? You’re literally a walking glowstick (no offense). A really attractive one in fact. 😊’

Marin felt his cheeks flush slightly.

But it quickly faded away as one of the cuts in his back throbbed painfully.

He winced, trying to change his position, but just made it worse

Trying to fight the pain, he quickly texted Aiden back

‘ You’re being extremely nice. Are you like this with everyone or just me?‘

Within 10 seconds, Aiden replied.

‘Everyone, TBH. But I talk more with people I’ve known for a while. I sent like 59 messages a day to them. I wait for new people (like you) to get used to my fast typing speed first.’

Marin it with a sigh as his back slowly stopped aching he replied…

‘Oh, I see. I’m getting tired though. I’ve gotta go to bed, talk to you later’

“Oh, K! Bye! See you in school on Monday!’ Aiden replied

As Marin turned off his phone, he got into a more comfortable position and eventually dozed off.

He had a dream.

It wasn’t any normal Dream

It was pitch black

But there was a light source

A woman

Marin went towards the light

She turned

He froze

“…maman…?” He whispered quietly

He wasn’t mistaken

It was his mother

Except… She was an angel

Ethereal , dressed in pristine white, silk robes which flowed elegantly as she moved.

Pearls and jewelry adorned her body.

"...mon petit garcon..." she replied, outstretching her hands for a hug.

But just as he was about to reach for her...

he woke up.

he jolted sharply as he woke up. he couldn't breathe

asthma attack

'again...?' he thought, reaching for the inhaler in his drawer.

he quickly used it, then went back to sleep, hoping to see his mother once again

but he didn't dream this time.

just slept.

The next morning, it was a Saturday. Marin didn’t wanna get up, but he couldn’t stand the taste of his own breath.

He hated his morning breath

He hated his morning headaches

And most of all, he hated the sound of Heather‘s voice in the morning

Unless she wasn’t home

He didn’t care about that yet, though

He trudged into the bathroom, and stripped

He didn’t look at his previous injuries in the mirror

He didn’t want to

He already knew what they’d look like

He just went to the sink and started brushing his teeth

When he was done, he put on his gloves and got into the shower, not the bathtub

As the warm water sprayed down, soaking him from head to toe, he sighed

His mind flashed back to the dream

His mom’s been dead for eight years

She died on Thanksgiving due to an asthma attack when Marin was seven

She’d been rushed to the hospital

Marin had asked his sister, Bella, “what happened to maman? Is she not feeling well?”

And his sister, who was 12 at the time, broke down in tears

He asked her what was wrong, and she just crouch low and patted his head, saying, ‘ it’s OK… She’s fine…maman’s fine…’

But when his mom didn’t show up for his eighth birthday a month later, or Bella’s 13th birthday two months later

He knew she wasn’t coming back

It hurt to remember

It hurt to look in the mirror

Because he looked exactly like her

And every time he looked in the mirror, he remembered

As he stepped out of the bathroom, robe on and tied securely, covering every inch of skin, except from the knee downwards

As he got a towel and proceeded to dry his hair, his phone dinged with a message

It was from his sister, Bella

But he called her ‘belle’. Because when he was younger, he couldn’t properly pronounce her name, so he just said “Belle”. it was easier to pronounce, and was French for ‘ beauty‘

Bella never minded, in fact, she loved to hear him call her that

Now she’s 21, and in college studying business, so she can take over their father’s company in the future

It wasn’t her dream profession, though. Her dream profession wants to be a doctor or a therapist, heck, even a teacher too. But her dad always admired the brains and beauty.

Since Bella was the first child, she’d be the one to take over the company, so he sent her to business school

Bella had a fiancé though. They were planning to marry after they finished college. They’ve been together for four years.

He picked up his phone, carefully removing one glove before checking the message

The message read...

"hey baby bro. what's up? how's school? is everything alright?"

He stared at the message.

Nothing was up.

Everything was not okay.

His life felt messed up.

yet he replied

'hey belle. i'm fine, its pretty boring over here without you tho. evertything's going pretty well over here though. how about you?? 😊'

He felt bad about lying

But he couldn't bear to know how she'd react to the fact that he's literally being physically abused by the very woman his dad married to keep an eye on him and make sure he didn't try to die again.

To take his mind off things, he took up a new hobby, reading.

He read to escape reality.

He buried himself in novels to feel a sense of belonging.

smut was for when he felt lonely.

suspense was for boring days.

horror was for stormy nights

fantasy was for when he wanted to learn about other realms.

fluff was for when he needed warmth and comfort.

But now?

he didn't know anymore.

he felt like ending it all

but he knew it wouldn't be his mother's wish for him to join her

she'd want him to live.

and be happy.

but he wasn't happy

in any way.

It was a saturday, so he wouldn't really be going anywhere.

when he finished dressing and texting his sister, he walked out of his room and went down the hall...to the room only he had the key to

He unlocked it, and stepped in.

It was filled to the brim with books, and was always warm.

He walked over to the large chair in the middle of the room, before picking up a book on the table in front of him and opening to the last bookmarked page.

"...withered lovers...chapter 35...page 208..." marin muttered to himself, flipping through the pages.

Marin then began to read, drifting off into the depths of the fictional world as the book drew him in deeper, until...his stomach grumbled.

Marin had been a vegetarian for 3 years, but quit due to certain...cravings.

cravings of meat...

chicken...

beef...

and a fresh-slaughtered, grass-fed, wagyu A5 cow paired with sushi, caviar and pancakes with whipped cream instead of syrup.

buttttttttttttttttttttttt...

he pushed it down.

his dad would grant his wishes without hesitation, but marin didn't want to tell him.

he felt like a burden, and that was a constant reminder that his life wasn't perfect.

By Monday, he got dressed in his winter school clothes

Winter was the only time he could wear long sleeves openly without it looking weird

Because he was the kind of kid who wears a hoodie all year round and never gets noticed

Turtlenecks and hoodies were all he wore to school

He was tired of it

He wanted to wear short sleeves, but cold weather made him feel sick to the core

But he could withstand any form of heat

And produce it too

As he stepped into school, warmth, and enveloping him due to the inbuilt heaters, he immediately put his guard up, only doing what mattered to him and his education

But as he was walking out out of his third class, he bumped into someone

But as he was walking out out of his third class, he bumped into someone

“Sorry…” He muttered quickly, before looking at who he bumped into

The person was tall, with brown hair, brown eyes, a tail which swished slowly and sharp, calculating gaze

“…Aiden…?“ Marin asked, raising an eyebrow.

“No. His brother.” the Person replied, folding his arms.

“Oh… So, Ethan then?” Marin asked

“Yeah. You are?”

“Marin”

“… I’ve never heard a name like that before”

“I know. It’s a unique one”

They stood there, and slightly awkward silence before Ethan walked away

Marin let out out of breath. He didn’t know he was holding.

Then his mind went to Aiden

You remembered Aiden’s voice, Aiden’s Confidence, Aiden’s –

He suddenly remembered

Aiden’s scarf!

He left it at home before coming to school. He was already internally panicking
